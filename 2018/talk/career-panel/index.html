Career panel at KPMG

Summary

On the evening of June 6th, BC Data Science workshop participants will gather at KPMG headquarters for a career panel and social event. The panel discusses non-academic career options for math-inclined folk; how to be prepared for industry jobs and job interviews; and what differences to expect in industry. More broadly, the panelists discussed career success, personal values, and strategies for achieving career-oriented goals.

The panel includes folk with a range of perspectives: from long-time industry folk to recent graduates in mathematics/statistics now working in industry. It includes current and past employees of major tech companies and current start-up leadership roles.

A reception follows from 7:00 to 8:00 PM.
